Sun Bak

Biography

Sun Bak (played by Doona Bae) is a businesswoman from Seoul who gets caught up in her family's embezzlement scheme. She's also a burgeoning star in the world of underground kickboxing. She learns of her connection to her Cluster when her and Lito both feel her period symptoms and visit one another throughout the experience.
