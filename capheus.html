Capheus Onyango

Biography

Capheus Onyango (played by Aml Ameen and Toby Onwumere) is a bus driver who knows every back alley of his native Nairobi. He's desperately trying to earn money for medicine for his dying mother. He learns of his connection to the Cluster after he gets caught up with a local gang and Will and Sun help him escape.
